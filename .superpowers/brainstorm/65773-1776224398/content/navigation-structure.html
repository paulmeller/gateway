How should the main navigation be organized? Think Vercel/Stripe left nav.

A

Resource-First Nav

Left sidebar is a vertical nav with sections: Overview, Agents, Environments, Sessions, Vaults, Files, Skills, Dashboard. Each section expands to show its list. Playground (chat) is one section, not the home page. Like Stripe's left nav.
